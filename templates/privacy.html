Data Privacy Policy

At EasyGoPharm, protecting your privacy is our priority. By using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your data as outlined below.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: name, email, phone number (when registering or submitting requests).
  • Medical Requests: medicine names or categories you search or request (never shared publicly).
  • Pharmacy Data: inventory details uploaded by registered pharmacies.
  • Usage Data: logs, cookies, and browsing activity to improve user experience.

How We Use Information

  • To connect patients/clinics with pharmacies.
  • To notify users when requested medicines are available.
  • To help pharmacies update and manage inventory visibility.
  • To improve platform performance, security, and services.

How We Protect Your Data

  • We use encryption, secure servers, and access controls.
  • Data is never sold to third parties.
  • Only pharmacies you choose to connect with can view your medicine requests.

Sharing of Data

  • With Pharmacies: Limited to medicine requests or search queries you initiate.
  • With Regulators: If required by law or regulatory authorities.
  • With Service Providers: For secure hosting and system support.

Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to deliver services or comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data at any time.
  • Opt out of notifications or delete your account.
  • Request full disclosure of how your data is used.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 18. If we discover we have collected data from a child without parental consent, we will delete it.

Compliance

We comply with Nigeria’s NDPR and follow global best practices (including GDPR principles) for data protection and security.

Contact

For privacy or data concerns, contact us at: easygo@easygopharm.com
